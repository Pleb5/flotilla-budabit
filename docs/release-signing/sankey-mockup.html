A Trust Chain Cards Low effort / Low risk

Each build is a card showing the trust chain vertically. Side-by-side comparison makes hash agreement immediately visible.

B Provenance Matrix Medium effort / Low risk

Rows are artifacts, columns are workers. Color-coded cells make outliers instantly visible. Scales to many builds.

C Trust Graph (2D Network) Medium-High effort / Medium risk

Layered DAG: Maintainers → Workers → Hashes → Artifacts. Edges show the trust chain. Hover highlights full provenance path.

D Sankey / Alluvial Flow Medium-High effort / Medium risk

One diagram per artifact. Flow width encodes consensus strength. Convergence = agreement, branching = disagreement.

E Hybrid — Graph + Detail Panel High effort / Medium risk

Trust graph on the left, click-to-inspect detail panel on the right. Best of both worlds for exploration and action.
